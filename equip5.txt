PROXXON PD 400 CNC LATHE MACHINE

The Proxxon PD 400 CNC is a precision lathe that automates machining via software, enabling repeatable operations. It handles tasks like turning, facing, drilling, taper-turning, knurling, threading, and parting off with high accuracy. Ideal for intricate projects, it ensures consistent results and versatility across multiple machining processes, making it a reliable choice for precision engineering needs.
